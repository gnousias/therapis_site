Therapis pro (prevention and physical wellness)

What is therapis pro?

Starting with an extensive and careful history, we record possible concerns, musculoskeletal or other health problems and continue with an assessment of strength and mobility throughout the body. We propose a full-body massage in which any technique is applied as the therapist deems appropriate (see techniques and technological equipment). From the data we have collected, we create a program that the patient can follow alone with the guidance of us or a specialized trainer / trainer, depending on the case, to avoid injuries and maintain proper posture in everyday life. We focus on the early detection of dysfunctions before they develop into painful conditions.
