Therapis pro (prevention and physical wellness)

What is therapis pro?

We begin with a thorough and detailed medical history to record any concerns, musculoskeletal or other health conditions, and continue with a full-body strength and mobility assessment. We recommend a full-body massage, during which the therapist applies any technique deemed appropriate (see techniques and technological equipment). Based on the collected data, we create a personalized program that the client can follow independently, with our guidance, or with a specialized fitness trainer/coach depending on the case. The goal is injury prevention and maintaining proper posture in daily life. We focus on the early detection of dysfunctions before they develop into painful conditions. Recommended for: professional and amateur athletes, professionals engaged in heavy manual labor, and as a gift card option (contact us).
