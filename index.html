Frequently asked questions

How long does each session last?

Typically, a session lasts between 45 to 60 minutes. This ensures enough time for both manual therapy and specialized exercises.

What should I wear to my first visit?

We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing or athletic wear. This allows our therapists to easily access the area being treated.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

While a referral is helpful, you can book an assessment directly with us. We will evaluate your condition and coordinate with your physician if necessary.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your specific condition and recovery goals. After the first evaluation, we will provide a personalized treatment plan.
