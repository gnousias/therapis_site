Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 29/03/2026

At Therapis Physio Center, we recognize the sensitivity of the information you entrust to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage your personal and health-related data.

1. Data Collection & Purpose

We collect the following information through our contact forms and consultation requests:

  • Identity Data: Full name and surname.
  • Contact Data: Phone number and email address.
  • Health Data: Specific conditions (e.g., back pain, injury history) and symptoms provided by you to help us prepare for your clinical assessment.

Purpose: We collect this data to provide accurate triage, to contact you for appointment scheduling, and to ensure your treatment plan is safe.

2. Processing of Sensitive Health Data

Under GDPR, information regarding your physical health is considered "Special Category Data". By submitting our contact form and describing your symptoms, you provide explicit consent for us to process this information solely for medical consultation purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored in secured digital environments with restricted access. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or health data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your data, ask for corrections, or request the deletion of your data ('Right to be Forgotten'), provided it does not conflict with legal medical record-keeping requirements in Greece.
