Therapeutic massage

What is therapeutic massage?

We begin with a detailed and careful medical history to record possible concerns, musculoskeletal or other health issues, identify any contraindications, and understand your needs. Through massage and targeted manual techniques, we aim to relieve muscle tension, improve blood circulation, and enhance tissue elasticity. You may choose a full-body massage or focus on a specific area (e.g., back only, legs only). Any technique selected by the therapist (see techniques and technological equipment) is applied at no extra cost. Therapeutic massage contributes to faster tissue recovery and immediate reduction of muscle fatigue.
