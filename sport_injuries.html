Sports injuries rehabilitation

What is sports injuries rehabilitation?

Specialized rehabilitation protocols for athletes of all levels. We focus on a safe and rapid return to sports activities. We support the athlete from the acute stage of injury through return to training and competition, with continuous re-evaluations. Comprehensive rehabilitation is provided using evidence-based protocols, including post-operative recovery.

Activforce 2 Digital Dynamometer & Inclinometer

Designed for objective assessment of muscle strength and range of motion in clinical practice, we measure the muscle strength and mobility of the targeted anatomical area. It provides graphical data related to muscle power, strength symmetry, maximum and average force values, allowing accurate monitoring of patient progress. Athletes may also use it for injury prevention (e.g., asymmetry screening), goal setting, and tracking rehabilitation progress.
